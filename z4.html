2 To 8°C Laboratory & Blood Bank Refrigerators

Product Description

Laboratory Refrigerator is based on Forced air Principal the evaporator air is forced into the cabinet by internal air circulation fan there by maintaining the cabinet temperature. Such systems are provided with time based defrost cycle. The Single compressor Refrigeration System the air cooled refrigeration system has been designed to maintain the set temperature in elevated ambient temperature environments. Single refrigeration system, air-cooled compressors. CFC/HCFC-free refrigerants. Multi-functional Controller. Protected security system for temperature & alarm settings. Independent operating temperatures and alarm functions. Temperature adjustments in of 0.1°C increments. The control system provided with Audible & visual alarms alert Hi-Low temperature alarms, Lack of Power, Battery Health, Door Open alarm etc. Low profile design. Controller Mounted at reachable level. Insulated Glass Doors or Blind Doors. Heavy door hinges & adjustable stainless-steel shelves, creating adjustable interior compartments. Laboratory freezers are used to store laboratory samples and reagents with precise temperature control.
